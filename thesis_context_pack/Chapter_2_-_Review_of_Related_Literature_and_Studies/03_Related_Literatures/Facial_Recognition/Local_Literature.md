# Facial Recognition - Local Literature

Domestic research brings out the use of face recognition by the police in the Philippines and the provision of services to the public. One research paper is an evaluation of open-source facial recognition models that are used for the identification of criminals by comparing model-based and appearance-based methods focusing on real-life situations like lighting changes and head movement. Besides, it maintains that the accuracy of the algorithms is significantly improved when they are trained with a large amount of data while the demand for adequate control is still emphasized (Rey et al., 2022). In addition, government records reveal the first facial recognition implementations at major international airports. at Ninoy Aquino International Airport, reports elaborate on how cameras were utilized to scan the passengers and link them with the national and global records, hence, the country is listed among the early biometrics border monitoring adopters (Santos, 2016).
According to the advisory released by the National Privacy Commission, facial pictures are regarded as sensitive personal data, and organizations need to acquire a lawful basis, apply transparency, observe proportionality and, when using biometric systems in private facilities, obtain consent. (National Privacy Commission, 2019). A subsequent advisory sets out the rules for local identity verification in the financial sector and includes provisions for data protection, secure storage, limited data sharing, privacy impact assessment, and organizational accountability (National Privacy Commission, 2023). More recent reports reveal government plans to employ biometric technology in upgrading airports and public services as a means to facilitate identity checks for travelers and OFWs. Such stories depict an extensive use of biometric systems across the country, with the attendant privacy and ethical issues that such a widespread adoption entails, being once again emphasized (Presidential Communications Office, 2025).
