# Daily Summary and Stability Analysis

The daily numbers of authorized recognitions, unknown detections, smoke-related events, fire alerts, and intruder-related alerts are recorded in a table.

The recommended treatment is descriptive:
- frequency counts per day
- percentage distribution when useful
- narrative comparison across repeated testing days

This part of the analysis may be used to discuss system stability, repetition of nuisance triggers, and overall monitoring behavior over time.
