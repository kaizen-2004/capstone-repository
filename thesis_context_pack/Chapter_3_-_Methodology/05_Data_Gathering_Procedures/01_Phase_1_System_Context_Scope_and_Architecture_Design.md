# Phase 1: System Context, Scope, and Architecture Design

Researchers first review the literature and then examine the condo-like environment to identify the threats that could be expected from intruders or fire. They design the system architecture, define the two monitored areas, identify the camera and sensor roles, and prepare the local monitoring workflow. At this phase, deployment options such as the use of Raspberry Pi 2 for local edge hosting are also considered. No formal numerical results are recorded at this point; the outputs are design documents, diagrams, deployment notes, and functional scope definitions.
