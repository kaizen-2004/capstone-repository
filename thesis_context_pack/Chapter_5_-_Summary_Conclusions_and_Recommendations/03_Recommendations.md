# Recommendations

## Suggested draft

Based on the expected direction of the study, the following recommendations may be considered:

1. Increase the number and diversity of controlled test runs before final manuscript submission so that the statistical treatment in Chapter 4 is supported by stronger repeated-trial data.
2. Improve face-recognition enrollment quality by capturing more representative samples under different lighting conditions, viewing angles, and subject poses.
3. Continue refining the smoke-first fire logic to minimize nuisance-trigger escalation while preserving early warning behavior.
4. Expand testing for low-light and night-vision conditions so that the system's real-world monitoring limits are more clearly documented.
5. Consider Raspberry Pi 2 for final local edge hosting only if it improves deployment practicality, presentation setup, or embedded-system alignment without degrading system responsiveness.
6. Explore future expansion to additional monitored areas, more sensing nodes, or richer mobile notification features after the baseline prototype is fully validated.
